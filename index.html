9th Annual
PostgreSQL Conference Europe

October 24-27 2017
Warsaw Poland

After last years visit to Tallinn, Estonia, PostgreSQL Conference Europe 2017 will be held at the Marriott Warsaw Hotel in downtown Warsaw.

The event will as usual host a day of instructor-led training followed by three days of world-class PostgreSQL presentations and a superb hallway track. Full website with more information will come soon.

Follow us on @pgconfeu or /PGConf.EU for the latest news.

PGConf.EU 2017 is organized by PostgreSQL Europe
